About

Get ready for a taste of the South. The Erie Street Kitchen in the Venice Cafe is bringing farm fresh local produce from your favorite artisan suppliers and farmers to downtown Kent for the finest in hot chicken, one of a kind sides, and seafood fun. This chef-driven and clean, local food-focused eatery will be a favorite for the whole family as well as the hottest spot to bring guests to impress.

Thank you to our local farmers and producers including: Busy B Bakery – Black Dog Acres, Kent – Oak Tree Hydroponic Farms, Akron – Goodell Family Farm, Mantua – Tierra Verde Farms, Kent – Pettit's Green Thumb Farm, Ravenna
